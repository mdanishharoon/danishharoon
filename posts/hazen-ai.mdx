## Overview

Spearheaded the design, development, and deployment of a full-stack, real-time monitoring and analytics platform. Leveraged **Next.js** for a dynamic dashboard, supported by a **.NET central server** and **FastAPI microservices**, processing camera feeds from a message queue to compute metrics and deliver interactive visualizations.

## Key Contributions

- **Data Pipeline Design**: Advised on and collaborated in the design of a data processing pipeline to calculate key traffic metrics (e.g., flow, congestion, speed) from heterogeneous high-volume data sources, including ingestion via APIs and WebSockets.

- **End-to-End Ownership**: Managed the project independently from concept to production, including architecture, deployment, infrastructure management, and system optimization, demonstrating full-stack ownership and operational expertise.
